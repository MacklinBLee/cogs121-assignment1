Welcome to gamr!


Gamr is a game-oriented chat-matchmaking interface that allows users from around the world to meet and discuss about their favorite games. This interface not only allows users to communicate with each other, but it allows them to form social bonds inside and outside of their games. One such game that benefits from this interface is Slither.io. Through our live-chat system, individuals who use Slither while on Gamr, will always have a forum where they can meet other avid players, and also have the potential to meet and team up with these players in the game.

How to Play Slither?

Slither takes a classic span on the game snake. To play, users must first choose a name. Next, users have the option of using either their trackpads or mice to control the directionality of their movements. The final feature of Slither is the space bar. By pressing the spacebar, users can speed up their movements. This however, comes at cost of losing some of the “snakes” acquired score.
